3 Steps To Grow Your Audience On Most Platforms

MAY 31, 2023 BY PROBABLYRAGING

Want to grow your audience? Follow these tips for creating engaging content and increasing your visibility on social media. From using visually appealing graphics and audio to being authentic and genuine, you'll be well on your way to attracting more subscribers. Plus, learn how to use keywords and tags effectively and how to cross promote your videos on social media to reach a wider audience. Don't miss these essential strategies for building a successful YouTube channel

Creating engaging, high-quality content is the most important factor in attracting and retaining an audience. To create content that resonates with your target audience, you should:

  • Know your audience: Research your target audience and understand what kind of content they are interested in. This will help you create content that is relevant and engaging to them.

  • Focus on a specific niche: Specialising in a specific niche will help you attract a dedicated audience who are interested in that particular topic.

  • Use engaging visuals and audio: Use visually appealing graphics, images, and video footage to make your content more engaging. Pay attention to audio quality and use clear, concise language to communicate your message effectively.

  • Be authentic and genuine: Viewers are more likely to engage with content that feels authentic and genuine. Be yourself and let your personality shine through in your content.

  • Tell a story: People are naturally drawn to stories, so try to incorporate storytelling elements into your videos to make them more engaging and memorable.

Using keywords and tags effectively can help make your videos more discoverable on most platforms, which can lead to more engagement. Here are a few tips for using keywords and tags effectively:

  • Use relevant keywords in your video titles, descriptions, and tags: Choose keywords that accurately describe your video and are relevant to your target audience. This will make it easier for people to find your content when they search for those keywords on each platform.

  • Use long-tail keywords: Long-tail keywords are more specific and less competitive than short-tail keywords. For example, "how to bake a chocolate cake" is a long-tail keyword, while "baking" is a short-tail keyword. Long-tail keywords can help your content rank higher in search results and attract more targeted traffic.

  • Use a mix of broad and specific keywords: In addition to using specific, long-tail keywords, it can also be helpful to include some more general, broad keywords in your titles, descriptions, and tags. This will help your content appear in a wider range of search results.

  • Use tags wisely: Most platforms allow you to add only a certain amount of characters in your tags field. Use this space wisely by including relevant keywords and phrases that accurately describe your content.

Cross promoting your videos on social media and other platforms can help increase the visibility of your channel and attract a broader audience. Here are a few ways you can promote your videos on social media:

  • Share your videos on your personal social media accounts: Share your videos on your personal social media accounts, such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram, to reach your existing followers and friends.

  • Use hashtags: Use relevant hashtags when sharing your videos on social media to make it easier for people to find your content.

  • Join relevant groups and communities: Join groups and communities on social media platforms that are relevant to your niche. This will allow you to share your videos with a wider audience and potentially attract more subscribers.

  • Collaborate with other creators: Collaborating with other creators can help expose your channel to their audience, which can lead to more subscribers.

  • Utilise paid promotion: If you have a budget, you can use paid promotion on social media platforms to increase the visibility of your videos and attract more subscribers.
